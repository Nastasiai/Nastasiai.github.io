About me

A creative soul deeply connected to nature. More tea and more travel!

My name is Nastasia, and I’m from a small village nestled next to the mountains in Central Asia, Kyrgyzstan. I have a passion for hiking, music, and adventurous travel. I’ve already explored many fascinating places across Asia, from South Korea to India and Nepal, as well as most of Central Asia. Now, as I study in Europe, I’m on a mission to visit as many countries as possible.

I love spending time in nature with family and friends, and I’m also passionate about art. This inspires me to create—whether through photography, videography, or simply noticing and appreciating the beauty around me. Currently, I’m pursuing a degree in Contemporary Communication at LCC International University in Klaipeda, Lithuania.

One of my most cherished hobbies is crafting herbal teas from herbs that I gather myself.
