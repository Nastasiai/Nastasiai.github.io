Javna Tea

Javna Tea is a project born from my love of herbal tea.

In my family, we have a tradition of collecting herbs and making aromatic tea. My father was always passionate about it, and as I grew older, it became my hobby too. Javna tea project began in 2020 when I spent a lot of time walking in the mountains, gathering herbs. I decided I wanted to share my tea with others.

I designed my own logo and packaging, and I also created an Instagram page where people can purchase various herbal tea blends. These include mint, St. John's Wort, oregano, thyme, chamomile, and many other herbs.
